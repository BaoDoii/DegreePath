# Degree Requirements – CSUEB Computer Science (Lower Division)

To complete the lower-division Computer Science requirements at CSU East Bay, a student must complete:

## Required Lower-Division CS Courses
- CS 101 – Computer Science I (4 units)
- CS 201 – Computer Science II (4 units)
- CS 211 – Discrete Structures (4 units)
- CS 221 – Computer Organization & Assembly Language (3 units)
- CS 230 – Computing & Social Responsibility (3 units)

## Math Requirement
- Math 130 – Calculus I (GE Area B4 or equivalent)

## Completion Rules
- All courses must be completed with a grade of C- or better
- All listed CS courses are required (no substitutions at lower division)
